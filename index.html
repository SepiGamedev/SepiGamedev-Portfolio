ABOUT ME:

Hello, my name is Sepi, and I’m a hobbyist game developer from Argentina. I like twisting familiar game genres and mechanics to make weird and unique 2D experiences. Most of my games are made with the Unity engine and programmed on C#. To make games I also use Gimp or Aseprite for the graphics, and Audacity or BFXR for the sound effects. The music of my games is made by the amazing Leonardo Muller-Rodriguez , a talented musician from the UK. My favorite aspect of game developing is the game design, but I also enjoy writing weird stories. I really love participating on gamejams and working with different limitations like a set color pallet, theme or mechanic. I also have a sweet tooth and LOVE eating cake. Although I’m most familiar with the C# language, I’m currently learning Python, HTML, CSS and Javascript. All my games are listed below, but you can also check my itch page HERE. It is nice to meet you, and I’m always happy to talk about games with anyone, you can add me on my discord: Sepi#8959. I hope you enjoy my weird little games!

MY GAMES:

The Flock is a colorful pixel art 'bird' shoot' em up about controlling a flock of various birds (each one with its own unique attack) to protect the rain forest from the angry non-flying birds. There are three different game modes to enjoy, try to beat your high score in all of them! It was made in one month for the Marathon Jam 2022 where it came out fifth. Play the game HERE.

Sheep annoyer is an incremental clicker game where you have to annoy a sheep. Use your annoying points to upgrade your annoying skills or to hire people that will annoy the sheep for you. This game was made in a week as a present to Junior Little for the Secret Gregory gamejam. Play the game HERE.

Only one life is a casual game about a spaceship, destroying asteroids and collecting coins. Or is it? Try to avoid all your responsibilities ignoring text messages, notifications and phone calls while you play your game and escape your problems. This game was in a week made for the GJL Game Parade Summer 2021 and got third on the "best story" category. Play the game HERE.

Embark yourself on a holy mission. Explore a bast land and meet its unique inhabitants. Solve mindblowing puzzles. Get 26 different achievements. Recover the holy land. This game was made in a week for the Black and White gamejam where it came 12 on creativity and 22 overall. Play the game HERE.

BLEARGH, monsters can't speedrun is a pixel art rage platformer with a twist. You control a monster that in time controls a ninja trying to steal the treasure. Try to beat your best times, but be careful not to die a lot or the monster will literally explode in rage! Play the game HERE.

Where Is That Ducking Bomb is a weird Highscore point and click game. Your pet duck has been kidnapped and has a bomb attached to it! Find the duck, find the clues and defuse the bomb to save the day! This game was made for the "I Can't Draw But Want to Make a Game" Gamejam winning the category for best music and sound effects. Play the game HERE.

Final Fanta-Three is a Highscore Minigame inspired by the classic Gameboy. With the gameboys 4 color palette, switch between three heroes, each with his own strengths and weaknesses to defeat the hordes of monsters and beat your high scores. This is my first game ever made and I still love it to this day. Most people say it's too hard... Most people are weak... Play the game HERE.
